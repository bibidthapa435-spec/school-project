Learn more about our vision, history, and academic landscape.

Welcome to Jaljala Secondary School

Welcome to Jaljala Secondary School, a center of academic excellence and character development. Established with the vision of providing quality education, our school is dedicated to nurturing responsible, confident, and skilled students.

We offer a supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to achieve their full potential through academics, extracurricular activities, and community engagement. With experienced teachers, modern teaching methods, and strong values, we strive to prepare our students for future success and lifelong learning.

At Jaljala Secondary School, we believe that education is the foundation of a brighter future, and we are committed to helping every student grow intellectually, socially, and morally.

General Information

Shree Jaljala Secondary School is a trusted, government-recognized community school located in the beautiful region of Sankhuwasabha, Nepal. We are dedicated to providing a transformative learning experience that goes beyond classroom instruction.

Academic Programs

We provide standard educational pathways under national curriculum guidelines, designed to foster academic excellence, personal growth, and practical capability.

Primary Level

Grade 1 to 5. Cultivating reading, writing, mathematical foundation, and creative development in supportive surroundings.

Lower Secondary

Grade 6 to 8. Developing critical logic, language proficiency, computing concepts, basic sciences, and social responsibility.

Secondary Level

Grade 9 to 10 (SEE) & +2 Programs. Directing advanced studies, mock-examinations, scientific experiments, and professional guidance.
