About Shree Jaljala Secondary School

Learn more about our vision, history, and academic landscape.

Welcome to Jaljala Secondary School

Welcome to Jaljala Secondary School, a center of academic excellence and character development. Established with the vision of providing quality education, our school is dedicated to nurturing responsible, confident, and skilled students.

We offer a supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to achieve their full potential through academics, extracurricular activities, and community engagement. With experienced teachers, modern teaching methods, and strong values, we strive to prepare our students for future success and lifelong learning.

At Jaljala Secondary School, we believe that education is the foundation of a brighter future, and we are committed to helping every student grow intellectually, socially, and morally.

General Information

Shree Jaljala Secondary School is a trusted, government-recognized community school located in the beautiful region of Sankhuwasabha, Nepal. We are dedicated to providing a transformative learning experience that goes beyond classroom instruction.

Academic Programs

We provide standard educational pathways under national curriculum guidelines, designed to foster academic excellence, personal growth, and practical capability.

Primary Level

Grade 1 to 5. Cultivating reading, writing, mathematical foundation, and creative development in supportive surroundings.

Lower Secondary

Grade 6 to 8. Developing critical logic, language proficiency, computing concepts, basic sciences, and social responsibility.

Secondary Level

Grade 9 to 10 (SEE) & +2 Programs. Directing advanced studies, mock-examinations, scientific experiments, and professional guidance.

Message from the Former Principal

I had the honor of serving as the Principal of SHREE JALJALA SECONDARY SCHOOL from 2070 to 2083. During my tenure, I worked closely with teachers, students, parents, and the community to promote academic excellence, discipline, and holistic student development. I believe that education is the foundation of a successful and responsible society. Our School Facilities

Providing high-standard amenities to support excellent learning, physical health, and personal development.

Academic Facility

Equipped with modern, well-furnished classrooms, a rich library stocked with diverse resources, and standard laboratories to encourage practical and interactive scientific learning.

  • Modern Classrooms
  • Well-Stocked Library
  • Practical Science Labs

Sport & Physical Development

Fostering team spirit and health through sports. Our students actively engage in games like Kabaddi, volleyball, football, and athletics on our spacious playground under guidance.

  • Large Playground
  • Sports Guidance
  • Traditional & Team Sports

Health & Safety Support

Ensuring a safe environment for all. We provide continuous first-aid medical support, dedicated student guidance, regular health checkups, and clean, purified drinking water.

  • First-Aid Support
  • Purified Drinking Water
  • Secure Premises

Environment

Surrounded by the natural beauty of Sankhuwasabha, we offer a peaceful, clean, and eco-friendly green atmosphere with student gardens that inspires daily academic focus.

  • Clean & Green Campus
  • Peaceful Surroundings
  • Beautiful Student Gardens
