Message from the Former Principal

I had the honor of serving as the Principal of SHREE JALJALA SECONDARY SCHOOL from 2070 to 2083. During my tenure, I worked closely with teachers, students, parents, and the community to promote academic excellence, discipline, and holistic student development. I believe that education is the foundation of a successful and responsible society. It was my privilege to contribute to the growth of the school by supporting quality teaching, encouraging innovation, and fostering a positive learning environment. I remain grateful for the opportunity to have been part of this institution's journey and wish the school continued success in shaping future generations.
I wish the institution continued success and a bright future.
